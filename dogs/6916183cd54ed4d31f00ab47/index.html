Male 3 - 3.5 YEARS

Hank the Handsome is a shy, emotionally tender soul who spent several weeks on the lam, wandering the community frightened and alone, and while we may never know his full story, we do know he is a dog with a gentle heart fighting to feel safe again. Once brought into care, this delicate boy began to shine in the sweetest ways—tentative tail wags, soft play bows, long naps in cozy corners, and even hiding his favorite bones as if saving treasures for a better tomorrow. Every day he reveals a little more of his goofy, loveable personality, reminding us why watching a scared street dog blossom is one of the greatest joys in rescue. Hank is ready for someone patient, calm, and kind who will give him the room to open up at his own pace and the love he has always deserved.
